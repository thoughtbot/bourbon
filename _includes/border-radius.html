Border Radius helpers View source

The border-radius helper mixins provide shortcuts for targeting sides. These mixins are supported in Bourbon v3.0+

{% highlight scss %} @include border-top-radius(5px); @include border-bottom-radius(5px); @include border-left-radius(5px); @include border-right-radius(5px); {% endhighlight %}

Deprecation Warning: The official border-radius mixins are deprecated and have been removed in the Bourbon 3.0. Here's why.
