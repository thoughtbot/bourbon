Radial Gradient

Takes up to 10 gradients. Position and shape are required. See also the background-image mixin.

{% highlight scss %} @include radial-gradient(50% 50%, circle cover, #1e5799, #3dc3d1); @include radial-gradient(50% 50%, circle cover, #eee 10%, #1e5799 30%, #efefef); {% endhighlight %}
